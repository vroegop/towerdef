Abilities & Special Weapons

Every dramatic weapon is the same boring thing under the hood: a cooldown that spawns a timed effect the sim ticks.

The effect model

Abilities don't get bespoke engines. They push an entry into state.effects[]; the sim ticks each effect every step (apply pull/damage, count down ttl); the UI just draws what's in the list. Because effects live in the authoritative state, they replay correctly in the offline sim too.

// sim/abilities.js
const ABILITIES = {
  blackhole:{ cd:22, dur:4,  cost:0, mk:(x,y,L)=>({kind:'blackhole',x,y,ttl:4,r:90,pull:160,dps:18*L}) },
  meteor:   { cd:15, dur:0,  cost:0, mk:(x,y,L)=>({kind:'meteor',x,y,ttl:1.2,r:70,dmg:120*L,fuse:1.0}) },
  golden:   { cd:90, dur:8,  cost:0, mk:(x,y,L)=>({kind:'golden',ttl:8,rewardMult:3+L}) },
};

function tickEffects(state, dt){
  for(const e of state.effects){
    e.ttl -= dt;
    if(e.kind==='blackhole') applyBlackhole(state, e, dt);
    if(e.kind==='meteor')   applyMeteor(state, e, dt);
    if(e.kind==='golden')   state.rewardMult = e.rewardMult;
  }
  state.effects = state.effects.filter(e => e.ttl > 0);
}
UI vs sim, again. The black hole's swirling accretion disk and screen warp are UI-only — invented from {x,y,r,ttl} and discarded on reload. The pull force and damage are sim-side, so a black hole that fired while you were offline still cleared the wave.

Live · black hole pulling the horde

Click the arena to drop a black hole anywhere. Enemies inside r are pulled toward the core (pull px/s, scaled by closeness) and take dps·dt damage. The warp ring and pulse are pure UI.

Example weapons (long upgrades, big payoffs — "The Tower" style)

◉ Black Hole

cd 22sdur 4sAoE pull+dot

Spawns a singularity that drags enemies inward and shreds them. Upgrades: radius, pull strength, dps, duration, –cooldown. A late "Event Horizon" tier instakills weak/avg tiers caught at the core.

☄ Meteor

cd 15sfused 1sburst AoE

Telegraphed impact after a 1s fuse — big upfront damage, rewards positioning the horde first (synergy with Black Hole). Upgrades: count (multi-strike), radius, damage, shorter fuse.

★ Golden Hero

cd 90sdur 8seco buff

For a few seconds, every kill is worth far more gold/xp. Very long, expensive upgrade path (more duration, higher multiplier, lower cooldown). Defines a greed playstyle.

⟲ Chain Lightning

cd 6sbouncessingle→multi

Arcs between nearby enemies, falloff per jump. Upgrades: jumps, falloff, damage. (Note: the visible bolt is UI; the damage events are sim.)

⬡ Frost Nova

cd 10sslowdefensive

Pulses out, slowing nearby enemies — buys breathing room and sets up Meteor. Upgrades: radius, slow %, duration. Synergy keystone "Juggernaut" ignores the self-immobility risk.

⟡ Turret Drone

summonpersistentauto-fire

Deploys an autonomous shooter that adds DPS without your input — great for offline gains. Upgrades: count, fire rate, drone hp.

Ability state & the offline question

Field (in sim state)Purpose
cooldowns[id]seconds remaining; ticks down in step(), so it keeps cycling offline
autoCast[id]player toggle — abilities self-fire when off-screen if enabled
levels[id]upgrade level → feeds the mk() scaling
effects[]live effects; ticked authoritatively, drawn decoratively
Design decision worth making early: do abilities auto-cast while offline? If yes, the offline sim must include an auto-cast policy ("fire Black Hole when ≥6 enemies within radius"). Keep the policy simple and conservative so offline never out-earns active play — otherwise players optimize for being away.
