What changed

This is an actual rigged, animated monster — Quaternius's CC0 "Ultimate Monsters" goblin — loaded as glTF and playing its real Walk clip. Several instances march on the hero, each rotating to face its heading, sized/tinted to suggest grunts vs a brute.

Because it's true 3D, facing is free at any angle (no baked frames), the legs animate via the skeleton, and lighting reacts as they turn. Swap Anim to see Run/Attack/Idle/Death — all from the same file.

Toon = cel bands (reads hand-drawn), Pixelate = render low-res + upscale to sit beside 2D pixel art. This is the same model you'd screenshot from N angles to bake a 36-direction sheet.

Model: "Ultimate Monsters" goblin by Quaternius · CC0 (public domain).
